What We Do at OSG Consulting

At OSG Consulting, we specialize in helping organizations navigate business transformation with confidence. With over 18 years of delivery leadership behind us, our team brings deep expertise in project management, ERP readiness, data migration, stakeholder engagement, and enterprise testing.

We’ve led successful transformation programs for clients in health tech, insurance, energy, telecom, and the public sector — including Saskatchewan Blue Cross, Cherry Health, Aucerna/Quorum, TELUS, Shaw, and Videotron.

Our approach is grounded in:

  • Strategic oversight and hands-on execution
  • Full lifecycle delivery across cloud platforms and legacy modernization
  • Collaborative project governance and vendor coordination
  • Agile, hybrid, and waterfall methodologies, tailored to client needs

We help your team deliver complex projects with confidence—while building internal capability, reducing risk, and driving lasting change.
