About OSG Consulting

OSG Consulting is a boutique consultancy based in Calgary, Alberta, specializing in business transformation, project delivery, and ERP implementation.

Our team brings decades of experience supporting complex initiatives across telecom, health tech, energy, insurance, and the public sector. From large-scale system integrations to change enablement and stakeholder management, we help organizations lead transformation with structure, agility, and clarity.

We combine the discipline of enterprise-grade delivery with the flexibility of a small firm — giving clients direct access to senior talent and tailored solutions that work.

Whether you're planning a major ERP rollout, modernizing legacy systems, or navigating a shift in business processes, we’re here to guide the journey from vision to execution.
