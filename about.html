About OSG Consulting

Founded by Alla Ovcharenko, OSG Consulting is a boutique firm based in Calgary, Alberta, specializing in business transformation, project leadership, and ERP implementation.

Alla brings over 18 years of cross-sector experience across telecom, health tech, and public sector organizations, having led complex programs involving system integrations, vendor coordination, change enablement, and stakeholder management.

OSG Consulting delivers enterprise-grade structure with small-firm agility, ensuring focused, senior-led execution from day one.
